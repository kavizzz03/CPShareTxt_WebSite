User Instructions

  • View Text: Click on the "Display Texts" button to view saved text entries as user buttons.
  • View Files: Click on the "Display Files" button to see saved files.
  • Delete Text: To delete a text entry, click the Delete button next to the username. You will be asked to confirm the deletion.
  • Protect Text: Use the Protection Toggle next to each entry to mark a text as protected. Protected texts are safe from auto-deletion for up to 1 month.
  • Auto Deletion: Unprotected texts are automatically deleted after 5 minutes of saving. Protected texts are kept safe for up to 1 month, after which they will be deleted automatically.
  • Copy Text: Use the Copy button to copy text to your clipboard quickly.
  • View Details: Click on a username button to view the full text details below.

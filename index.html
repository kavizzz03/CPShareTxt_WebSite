How to Use CP Share TXT

  • Enter your username in the username field. This helps identify your submissions.
  • Select the input type: Text to submit plain text or File to upload a file.
  • If you select Text, type or paste your text (up to 10,000 characters) in the text box.
  • If you select File, click or drag & drop a file (max 10 MB) into the upload area.
  • Click the Share button to save your text or file securely.
  • Your shared data will auto-delete after 5 minutes to protect your privacy.
  • To provide feedback, use the Give Feedback button below the form.
